Presidential Entropy

Shannon entropy of Polymarket's distribution over candidates for the 2027 French presidential election — how undecided the race is, over time.

Overlay

Method: each candidate's probability is its Polymarket "Yes" price; the named candidates' prices are renormalized when they sum above 1, and a synthetic "Other" mass 1 − Σp is added when they sum below 1. Entropy is computed over that distribution. The four units are transforms of one another (bits = nats / ln2, effective candidates = e^nats).
